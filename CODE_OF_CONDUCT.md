# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep collaboration professional, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

Acceptable behavior includes:

- using inclusive and precise language
- giving actionable technical feedback
- respecting different experience levels
- assuming good intent while still reviewing rigorously
- keeping discussions focused on the work

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or sexualized language or imagery
- publishing private information without permission
- intentionally derailing technical discussions
- pressuring others to disclose credentials, account data, or private trading information

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject PRs, or restrict participation when behavior violates this standard.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's maintainer contact path. If no private contact path is configured yet, open a short issue requesting a private maintainer contact without including sensitive details.
